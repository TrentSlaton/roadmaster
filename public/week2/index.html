Pre trip inspection

In-Cab

  1. three points of contact

  2. seatbelt - PMS (top, bottom, floor). not ripped, torn or frayed. clicks on, off, on.

  3. safe start - sweep: yellow/red buttons out, neutral, turn key 1 click - dash lights (blue DEF light, yellow ABS light) and trailer ABS light will illuminate then extinguish. Crank engine.

  4. emergency equipment

    1. 2 fire extinguishers - PMS, ABC rated, fully charge (needle in green), has safety pin
    2. 3 safety triangles - PMS, red and orange in color, they stand on their own
    3. spare fuses - i have a spare fuse for every fuse in the truck

  5. glass

    1. 6 mirrors, power windows, windshield - PMS, NCB, clean and clear in color, no illegal stickers, nothing obstructing my view
    2. power windows go up and down
    3. mirrors are adjusted to me

  6. window seals - PMS, not RTD, i see no leaks

  7. windshield wipers + blades - PMS, NCB, blades not RTD, blades pressed against windshield, (press button) they work and they spray

  8. heater + defroster - 3 dials: all the way, all the way, 2 o’clock. place hand by front, side, lower vents to feel the moving air. “my heater and defroster are working properly”

  9. lights - turn on headlights. green dash light = low beams, blue dash light = high beams. turn high beams on/off. left/right turn signals, 4 way hazards

  10. warning devices - city horn, highway horn

  11. 5 gauges

    1. oil pressure gauge - working properly (30-60psi)
    2. DEF fuel gauge - working properly. no less than 1/8 of a tank or i must refuel
    3. voltmeter - working properly (12-15V)
    4. air pressure gauge - working properly (120-140psi or until governor shutoff)
    5. water temperature gauge - working properly (180-190 degrees Fahrenheit)

  12. TTS tug test (Trailer, Truck, Service)

    • Trailer
      1. yellow button in, leave red out
      2. put truck in drive
      3. give a little bit of gas
      4. feel tugging on trailer
    • Truck
      1. pull yellow out, push red in
      2. give a little bit of gas
      3. truck should not move forward
    • Service
      1. push both buttons in
      2. accelerate to 3-5mph
      3. let go of steering wheel and gently apply brake
      4. when truck comes to a complete stop: “my truck did not go to the left or to the right, my service brakes are working properly”

  13. LABS - explain to the examiner exactly what you’re doing at every step

    1. build air pressure back up (120-140psi or until governor shutoff)
    2. turn off engine (1 click)
    3. turn key back one click (to get your dash lights)
    • Leaks - should not lose more than 4psi in 1 minute
      1. press and hold service brake until needle stabilizes on air pressure gauge
      2. start 1 minute timer
        1. at 20 seconds - “i do not see or hear any leaks”
        2. at 40 seconds - “i do not see or hear any leaks”
        3. at 1 minute - “in 1 minute, i did not lose more than 4psi. i did not see or hear any leaks”
    • Alarms - low air pressure warning alarm should come on at 55psi or greater
      1. pump service brakes until alarm comes on then stop
      2. when your alarms come on - “my alarms came on at 55psi or greater, i hear and see my alarms”
    • Buttons - both buttons should pop between 45-20psi
      1. pump service brakes watching your buttons not your gauges
      2. stop pumping when both buttons pop
      3. when both buttons have popped - “my buttons have popped between 45 and 20psi”

  14. turn engine on and say “im building my air pressure back to 120-140psi or until governor shutoff. in the meantime, if you could assist me with the lights, starting up front please”

Outside Truck and Trailer

  1. no puddles underneath the truck
  2. truck is level and is not leaning to the left or to the right
  • Truck
    • Lenses
      1. clearance light lenses - PMS, NCB, NMH, amber in color, clean
      2. both headlight lenses - PMS, NCB, clear in color, clean * they house the low beams, hight beams, turn signals, side reflectors
      3. side multi-purpose light lenses - PMS, NCB, NMH, amber in color, clean
      4. tail light lenses - PMS, NCB, NMH, red in color, clean
      5. reverse light lens - PMS, NCB, NMH, clear in color, clean

Notes

  • Don’t forget about your multi-color air hoses
  • Do not do service brakes before tug test
